Top Eco‑Friendly Cat Products

Treat your feline to sustainable gear and reduce your carbon pawprint.

Cats can be picky about their toys and accessories, but many eco‑friendly options now match or exceed the quality of conventional products. Consider biodegradable litter made from wood, corn or wheat that breaks down naturally and controls odours without added chemicals. Pair this with litter boxes and scoops made from recycled plastic.

Choose cat toys crafted from natural fibres like sisal, cork and wool. Not only do these materials satisfy your cat’s instinct to hunt and scratch, they’re also safe if chewed or shredded. Look for scratching posts and trees made from untreated wood and cardboard, which can be recycled when worn out.

Other eco‑friendly products include bamboo grooming tools, stainless steel bowls and recycled PET beds. By choosing green alternatives, you’ll ensure your cat lives comfortably while minimizing waste and environmental impact.
