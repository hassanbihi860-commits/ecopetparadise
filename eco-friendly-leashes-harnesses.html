Eco‑Friendly Leashes & Harnesses

Secure, comfortable gear made from sustainable materials.

Leashes and harnesses are everyday essentials for pet owners, yet many are made from petroleum‑based materials that contribute to pollution. Eco‑friendly alternatives use recycled polyester, organic cotton or hemp to create strong, durable straps that feel good in your hand and on your pet. Some brands even incorporate upcycled climbing rope or plastic bottles.

Choose hardware made from stainless steel or aluminium for longevity and rust resistance. Look for adjustable harnesses that distribute pressure evenly and reduce strain on your pet’s neck. Reflective stitching or accents enhance visibility during evening walks without needing batteries or extra gadgets.

When your gear reaches the end of its life, check if the manufacturer offers a recycling program. Opting for sustainable leashes and harnesses reduces waste and supports companies that prioritise earth‑friendly manufacturing.
