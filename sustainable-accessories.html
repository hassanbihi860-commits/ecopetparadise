Must‑Have Sustainable Accessories

Accessories that make sustainable pet parenting simple.

Accessories are often overlooked when switching to an eco‑friendly lifestyle, yet they can have a big impact on your pet’s environmental pawprint. Start with a sturdy poop bag dispenser made from compostable or biodegradable materials, and use certified compostable bags to safely dispose of waste.

Choose grooming tools made of bamboo or recycled plastic, which are lightweight and durable. For feeding, stainless steel or ceramic bowls are long‑lasting and easy to clean, reducing the need to replace cracked or stained plastic bowls. Collapsible silicone bowls are perfect for travel and can be reused endlessly.

Other useful accessories include leashes and collars made from recycled polyester, natural fibre bandanas, and beds filled with recycled or organic stuffing. Investing in these essentials not only reduces waste but also supports companies committed to ethical and sustainable manufacturing.
