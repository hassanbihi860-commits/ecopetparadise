DIY Eco‑Friendly Cat Toys

Craft engaging toys using upcycled household items and natural materials.

Making your own cat toys is a fun way to reduce waste and personalise playtime. Many household items can be transformed into irresistible playthings. For example, tie strips of old T‑shirts onto a stick to create a colorful wand toy. Cardboard toilet paper rolls can be cut and shaped into balls or folded into treat puzzles.

Fill small fabric scraps with dried catnip or silver vine and sew them shut to create homemade catnip pillows. For a natural scratching pad, glue layers of corrugated cardboard together and wrap the edges in jute twine. Always supervise your cat during play and choose safe materials that won’t splinter or unravel easily.

DIY projects not only spark creativity but also save money and reduce the need for new resources. Upcycling materials gives them a second life and keeps them out of landfills, making playtime more eco‑friendly for both you and your feline friend.
